Mobilizing Communities Across Kano State

The Kano Vorter Data Collection Point is a civic initiative ensuring every citizen in Kano State is registered and ready to participate fully in the democratic process.

Field registration ambassadors are deployed across all 44 LGAs to collect voter information, track registration status, and monitor progress in real time through our digital platform.

Our outreach targets all segments of the population including residents, market communities, and tertiary institution students and staff across Kano State.

Administrators generate reports, monitor center-level activity, and ensure no eligible voter is left behind.
